# 🏗️ Welcome to Data Science & Statistical Analysis with Python

**Instructor:** RUN Savin  
**Course Vision:** To move beyond just "writing code" and master the art of turning raw data into scientific proof for high-level business decision-making.

---

## 🗺️ Your Learning Roadmap

We will journey through **5 Core Modules**, taking you from a beginner to a Professional with working with Data.

---

### Module 01 · Data Science Foundation

| Chapter | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Chapter 1 | **Introduction to Data Science** — Understanding why data is the "new currency" and the critical difference between AI and Data Science |
| Chapter 2 | **Data Science Methodology & Business Understanding** — Mastering the Data Science Lifecycle and learning to turn messy business problems into clear technical questions |

---

### Module 02 · Programing for Data Science

| Chapter | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Chapter 3 | **Python for Data Science** — Mastering **Pandas** and **NumPy** to clean, manage, and organize real-world datasets that are often incomplete or "dirty" |
| Chapter 4 | **Visualization with Python** — Using **Seaborn** and **Matplotlib** to create modern infographics that tell a clear story with numbers |

---

### Module 03 · Probability & Statistical Analysis

| Chapter | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Chapter 5 | **Descriptive Statistics & Visualization** — Summarizing and visualizing data distributions to uncover patterns and key insights |
| Chapter 6 | **Multivariate Analysis** — Moving beyond simple charts to see how multiple variables interact and learning to spot logical traps like **Simpson's Paradox** |
| Chapter 7 | **Probability & Sampling** — Learning the math of randomness — how to ensure your data is fair, unbiased, and representative of the real world |

---

### Module 04 · Statistical Inference & Testing

| Chapter | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Chapter 8 | **Statistical Inference** — Learning how to predict what a whole population thinks by looking at a small group using **Confidence Intervals** |
| Chapter 9 | **Hypothesis Testing** — The scientific framework: using **p-values** to prove if your results are a true discovery or just a lucky accident |

---

### 🎓 Module 05 · Project Roadmap, Report & Presentation

| | |
|-|-|
| **The Final Goal** | You will choose a real-world dataset, perform a full end-to-end analysis, and present your findings like a professional Data Scientist |

## 🛠️ What You Need to Succeed

We focus on three key pillars throughout this course:

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 🧠 **The Logic** (Analytical Thinking) | Developing "Data Literacy" so you can think critically before you even start typing code |
| 🔧 **The Tools** (Hands-on Python) | Getting comfortable with **Jupyter Notebooks** and professional statistical libraries |
| 🎯 **The Application** (Impact) | Moving from *"Data-Informed"* (having data) to **"Data-Driven"** (using data to make the right move) |

---

## 📈 Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- ✅ Think critically and challenge data-driven claims
- ✅ Apply the full data science process to any real problem
- ✅ Write clean Python code to analyze and visualize data
- ✅ Run descriptive and inferential statistics with confidence
- ✅ Design and interpret hypothesis tests
- ✅ Present your findings clearly to any audience — technical or not
